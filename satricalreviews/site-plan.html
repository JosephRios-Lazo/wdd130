Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide readers with entertaining reviews. The goal is to take out the boredom in reading reviews about new products to buy.

Audience

The target audience is 20-45 age range. The goal is to relate to as many people as possible with these reviews.

Content

Home page

I'll have a heading with links to each of the pages in the top right corner. Footer will also have links to each page.I will have logo in top left corner next to the company name. There will be a big hero image related to reviewing or satire. Below the hero are three sections with images speaking about what the site offers.

Images for the Home page

Reviews

There will be a list of reviews on this page each connected to the product page via a link. Footer and heading will be the same as the home. This page will have a lot of room for reviews.

Images for the Reviews

Products

This is where the products that have reviews will be listed. This will serve as a sort of navigation page that allows direct links to the reviews on the review page.
